NOTE: inning --> An inning is the period during which a team takes its turn to bat.

1) A batsman makes a score of 87 runs in the 17th inning and thus increases his average by 3. Find his average after the 17th inning.

54
39
27
41

  
ANSWER --  39
Let the average after 17th inning = x
Then average after 16th inning = x - 3
16(x-3)+87 = 17x 
x = 87 - 48 = 39 

Average = (Total Runs) ÷ (Number of Innings)
   x = [16(x -3) + 87] ÷ 17
